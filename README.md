## JW Helper

### App, that simplifies the work that brothers, responsible for assigning tasks to others, have to do.

### App lets you: 
- choose what brothers to assign
- choose the roles of those brothers
- choose the availability of those brothers
- automatically and fairly distribute tasks for them
